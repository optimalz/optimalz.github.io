2015-12-21 Christmas Party

Today the annual Christmas party of the Center for Medical Physics and Biomedical Engineering took place in the Klosterneuburg Monastery. 
The evening started with chestnut and hot wine infront of the Monastery, followed by an impressive pipe organ concert. 
After that a large and delicious buffet was offered for dinner. The evening was finish off with some drinks and nice conversations. 
In this sense, we want to wish everybody a merry Christmas and a happy new year.
