New article on longitudinal monitoring of spontaneous retinal neovascularization published in IOVS


24/04/2018

We just published a longitudinal investigation of the early development of spontaneous retinal neovascularization in Investigative Ophthalmology and Visual Science (IOVS). Using a multifunctional optical coherence tomography approach, we characterized tissue changes in vivo and described structural and functional changes in the murine retina over time. Read more in the article here:
