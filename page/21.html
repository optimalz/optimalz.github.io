New article demonstrates the use of visible-light OCM imaging and spectroscopy for characterizing Alzheimer's disease in brain tissue samples.


07/08/2017

If you want to find out more about our newly developed spectral domain visible light optical coherence microscopy system, have a look at our recent publication in Biomedical Optics Express which is also featured in OCT News. We investigated amyloid-beta plaques of various sizes in human brain tissue and animal models of Alzheimer's disease. We performed spectroscopic and attenuation analysis of these data. Furthermore a first effort was made to investigate optically cleared mouse brain tissue to increase the penetration depth.
