Ocular fundus pulsations within the posterior rat eye: Chorioscleral motion and response to elevated intraocular pressure. New article published now online in Scientific Reports.


18/08/2017

The new article published in Scientific Reports shows a quantitative evaluation of elevated intraocular presure in albino rats, indicating that the assessed pulsatility may be a avluable parameter for describing the choroidal perfusion. Using a multi-functional optical coherence tomography approach it is possible to determine the ocular fundus pulsations and investigate changes in the retina related to high intraocular pressure.
