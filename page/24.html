Simon Mastered Master of Science


23/10/2017

Simon successfully defended his Master thesis entitled Mehrschichtige Streuphantome für die optische Kohärenztomographie (Multi-layer scattering phantoms for OCT) at the Vienna University of Technology. The work was conducted in a cooperation between Vienna University of Technology (Institute for Applied Physics, Institute of Chemical Technologies and Analytics) and the Medical University of Vienna. We wish Simon all the best for his future!
