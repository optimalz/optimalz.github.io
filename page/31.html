New article on BRAD-OCT for neuroimaging applications published on Biomedical Optics Express


02/05/2018

Our novel detection concept for detecting the bright and dark field in OCT systems simultaneously has been recently published in BOE. By exploding the capabilities of few-mode fibers, we demonstrate the posibility of collecting the backscattering signal with different detection profiles, amplifying the scattering characterization of samples in neurological applications.
