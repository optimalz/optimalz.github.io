New article published in Optics Letters


13/08/2019

Our advances regarding the novel technique of BRAD-OCT imaging have been published in Optics Letters. In this paper, we present a polarization-sensitive extension to our previous system which expands the contrast capabilities of investigating biological scattering properties with OCT. The full article can be found here: https://doi.org/10.1364/OL.44.004040
