New article published in the Journal of Biophotonics


13/08/2019

Our latest manuscript describes a hyperspectral extension to white light optical coherence tomography, allowing the identification of individual particles based on their scattering properties. This manuscript presents theoretical simulations, phantom measurements and an in vivo application: visualization of melanin in the low concentration Brown Norway rat retinal pigment epithelium. The full, open access manuscript can be found here: https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/epdf/10.1002/jbio.201900153
