Schrödinger fellowship


06/04/2020

Antonia was awarded with the Schrödinger fellowship. This fellowship will give her the great opportunity to conduct research for one and a half years at the university of Tsukuba in Prof. Yasuno's group and finally return to the Centre for Medical Physics and Biomedical engineering in 2021 for half a year. Her project, entitled "Non-invasive ex-vivo/in-vivo pre-clinical tissue analysis platform" will improve and advance ex-vivo and in-vivo pre-clinical tissue analysis using multimodal optical coherence microscopy. The platform will facilitate investigations in three major pre-clinical scientific fields for cancer research, ex-vivo tissue analysis, in-vivo/in-vitro cell culture and animal model research.
